    Facts about blue sharks

  • The blue shark species can be found in Cornwall, during summer months then migrates to warmer climates in the later months of the year

  • The sharks have been spotted to grow up to 10 feet some even reaching 12 feet

  • They are pelagic feeders who feed on sea dwellers such as herring, squid and mackerel.Though they sometimes prey on white fish such as pollock and cod.
